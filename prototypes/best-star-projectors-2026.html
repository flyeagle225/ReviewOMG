FAQ

What is the best star projector for most people?

A flexible galaxy projector is best for most bedrooms. A home planetarium is better if the user cares about realistic stars.

Are star projectors safe for kids?

They can be appropriate when used as directed, but parents should check brightness, timer, heat, small parts, and laser-related claims.

What is the difference between a galaxy projector and an ocean projector?

A galaxy projector focuses on stars and nebula effects. An ocean projector focuses on wave-like movement and calm blue ambience.
